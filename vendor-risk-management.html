Key Benefits

Automated Vendor Discovery: Instantly identify all vendors—including shadow IT—across your environment for complete risk visibility.

Continuous Third-Party Monitoring : Proactively monitor all vendors, get real-time alerts on changes or new threats, and ensure compliance at every stage.

Automated Due Diligence & Evidence Collection : Send evidence requests, automate reminders, and manage document intake—no more chasing vendors over email or spreadsheets.

Risk Scoring & Custom Rubrics: Score vendors based on inherent and residual risks using out-of-the-box templates or customizable rubrics tailored to your risk appetite.

Integrated Remediation & Follow-Up: Assign owners, set response timelines, and track remediation of identified vendor risks directly within the platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does TRAC support vendor risk scores and custom attributes?

Yes—TRAC allows both automatic and manual risk scoring using customizable rubrics. You can define and track any field needed for your program.

Can I automate evidence requests and follow-ups?

Absolutely. TRAC’s workflow engine automates outreach and follow-ups, so nothing falls through the cracks.

How does TRAC help identify shadow IT and unsanctioned vendors?

TRAC leverages environment integrations to auto-discover vendors, flagging any that aren’t on your approved list for further review.

Can I build custom questionnaires?

Yes—use pre-built templates or design your own with dynamic fields for targeted due diligence.

How do real-time alerts work?

Get notified of vendor status changes, documentation gaps, and open risks—prioritized for your team to take action immediately.

Does TRAC integrate with procurement systems?

TRAC’s modular integrations ensure seamless workflow between risk reviews, procurement, and compliance documentation.
