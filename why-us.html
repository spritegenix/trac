Why Choose TRAC?

Because compliance shouldn’t be complicated

Managing cybersecurity compliance is more critical and more challenging than ever. Manual processes, fragmented tools, and ever-changing regulations make staying compliant a full-time job for already stretched teams.

TRAC changes that, It’s a unified, automated platform that simplifies compliance and risk management so you can focus on what truly matters — securing your business.

The Compliance Landscape Today

58% of organizations say compliance processes are overly manual and time-consuming.

It takes an average of 1,800 hours annually for mid-sized firms to maintain compliance with frameworks like ISO 27001 or SOC 2.

83% of companies experience audit delays due to poor evidence management and documentation silos.

TRAC is designed to eliminate that waste and the worry.

Built by Security Experts, Designed for Simplicity

Our team has helped organizations across industries achieve and sustain compliance. With TRAC, you get that expertise baked into an intuitive platform — no steep learning curve, no unnecessary complexity.

Ready for Any Framework

Whether you're tackling ISO 27001, SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR, or PCI DSS, TRAC keeps your controls aligned and your reporting streamlined. One platform, many standards.

Always Audit-Ready

With centralized documentation, continuous monitoring, and real-time dashboards, TRAC keeps you ready for internal and external audits — not just once a year, but all year round.

Supported by Humans Who Understand Security

From onboarding to implementation, our team works as your extended compliance partner — not just a helpdesk. We’re responsive, knowledgeable, and easy to reach.

Automation Where It Matters Most

TRAC automates the heavy lifting:

  • Risk scoring and mitigation tracking
  • Control mapping across multiple frameworks
  • Policy creation, distribution, and acknowledgment
  • Evidence collection and audit report generation

Less manual work, fewer errors, and more time for strategic initiatives.

Compliance, without the chaos

If you’re spending too much time preparing for audits, juggling frameworks, or chasing documentation — it’s time for a smarter way.
