Connected Risk & Compliance for Tech Leaders

Modern technology companies must balance speed and innovation with ever-evolving regulatory, privacy, and security demands. TRAC centralizes your GRC activitiesenabling digital businesses, SaaS providers, developers, and enterprise IT teams to integrate risk management into every stage of their value chain.

How TRAC Accelerates Technology GRC

Centralized Controls & Policy Automation

  • Deploy, manage, and track policies, SOPs, and controls across your tech stack and global operations from a single platform.
  • Instantly update controls in response to new regulations or security threats.

Security-First Culture

  • Deliver role-based compliance and secure coding training.
  • Monitor user participation and policy acknowledgments for regulatory defensibility.
  • Support global languages and formats to engage distributed teams efficiently.

Horizon Scanning & Risk Monitoring

  • Scan global sources for emerging riskscyber, regulatory, operational.
  • Receive dynamic risk scores and notifications, enabling nimble, timely decisions.

IT & Cyber Risk Integration

  • Map cyber, data, and infrastructure risks directly to business and product objectives.
  • Automate assessments and reporting for every asset, application, and cloud instance.

Vendor & Third-Party Risk Management

  • Standardize onboarding, monitoring, and oversight for vendors, partners, and platforms.
  • Integrate external risk intelligence and enforce compliance throughout your supply chain.

Key Capabilities

Centralized Risk Oversight: Aggregate operational, cyber, and compliance risk data from all products and processes, mapped to your business goals.

Proactive Vendor & Cloud Risk Monitoring: Evaluate third-party, supply chain, and cloud provider risk using automated assessments and live dashboards.

Real-Time Audit Readiness: Maintain continuous compliance across frameworks such as GDPR, CCPA, DPDP, SOC 2, ISO 27001, NIST, and morewith real-time status tracking and alerts.

Automated Control & Threat Mapping: Align technical and organizational controls to identified threats, mapping them to leading frameworks including NIST and ISO for streamlined compliance.

AI-Driven Security Insights: Detect IP misuse, data leaks, or anomalous access with smart, automated risk signals and analytics.
