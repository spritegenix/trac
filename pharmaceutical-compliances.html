Core Regulatory Frameworks for Pharmaceutical Industry

Pharma organizations must adhere to a wide range of global and regional regulatory mandates, including:

Compliance/Standard Description
GxP (GMP, GLP, GCP) Good Practice standards for manufacturing, lab, and clinical processes
FDA 21 CFR Parts 11, 210, 211 US requirements for electronic records/signatures, drug manufacturing, and product controls
ICH Q7 Guidelines on Good Manufacturing Practice for APIs, globally recognized
ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001 Standards for quality, environmental, and workplace safety management
EU GMP, WHO GMP Regulatory frameworks for European and global pharmaceutical manufacturing
CDSCO, Drugs and Cosmetics Act Indian pharma compliance (Central Drugs Standard Control Organization)
GDPR Data protection regulation relevant to patient and clinical trial data in the EU
DPCO, NPPA Drug pricing and market compliance in India
Environmental Protection Act Environmental impact and hazardous waste management
