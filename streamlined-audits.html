Frequently Asked Questions

How does TRAC keep us audit-ready throughout the year?

TRAC runs 24/7 controls checks and pulls new evidence from your systems, so your compliance status and documentation are always up to date.

Can we choose what data auditors can access?

Absolutely. TRAC’s permissions let you share only relevant evidence and reports—protecting sensitive or out-of-scope information.

Do auditors need training to use TRAC?

No. TRAC offers an intuitive, auditor-friendly interface and role-based access, so outside auditors can review, comment, and sign off with zero friction.

Can we reuse evidence across multiple audits and frameworks?

Yes. All uploaded and automated evidence is centrally stored and can be linked to controls across frameworks, avoiding duplication.

How quickly can we complete an audit using TRAC?

Most organizations reduce audit prep time by at least 50%, with some completing audits in a fraction of the previous time due to automation and live collaboration.
