Phishing Simulation

Let’s Find Out Who Would Click That Link Before an Attacker Does

You’ve trained your team. They get cybersecurity... or so you hope. But how do you really know if someone might still fall for a well-crafted phishing email?

That’s where the TRAP Phishing Simulation comes in. It’s a safe, realistic way to test what your employees have learned—without the actual risk of a breach.

What Is It?

The TRAP Phishing Simulation is like a fire drill for email threats.

 

We send out realistic-looking (but harmless) phishing emails to your employees to see how they respond. Who clicks? Who reports it? Who ignores it?

 

It’s not about embarrassing anyone—it’s about understanding where the gaps are and helping your team get even better.

Real-Looking Emails. Real Reactions. No Real Danger.

Our simulation uses carefully designed phishing emails that look and feel like the real thing

Fake login pages
Spoofed sender names
“Urgent” requests or fake invoices
And other tricks real attackers use every day
Audit-ready exports

You can choose from a variety of templates or we’ll create something specific to your industry or company.

Get the Full Picture with a Live Dashboard

You’ll get access to a private dashboard that shows:
  • Who opened the email
  • Who clicked the link
  • Who reported it
  • Who needs a bit more guidance

No finger-pointing—just clear insights to help your team grow.

Learn, Test, Improve. Repeat

The real magic happens when simulations are run regularly. Why?

Click rates drop over time
Awareness becomes habit
Employees stay sharp and vigilant

It’s a simple, powerful way to reinforce what your team learned during TRAP training and make sure they’re ready when the real thing shows up.

Included With TRAP Training

Every TRAP session includes at least one phishing simulation—because learning isn’t complete without practice. Want to run simulations more regularly? We’ve got you covered with quarterly or custom plans too.

Because Even Smart People Click Sometimes

We’ve seen it all: CEOs click, tech leads click, even security admins have clicked. The goal isn’t perfection—it’s progress. TRAP Phishing Simulation helps your team learn safely from mistakes before they’re costly.

Ready to See It in Action?

Let’s run a simulation and see how your team holds up. No judgment—just insights and improvement.
