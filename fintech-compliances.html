Instantly Address FINRA, RBI, and Global Standards:

Gap Analysis Made Simple
  • Proactively identify, assess, and remediate compliance gaps.
  • Comprehensive coverage for regulations such as FINRA, Reserve Bank of India (RBI), Federal Financial Institutions Examination Council (FFIEC), PCI DSS, GLBA, ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, and others.
  • Manage compliance from a single, unified workspace to reduce operational complexity.
Continuous Program Updates
  • Stay ahead with automated monitoring of regulatory changes.
  • Policy updates and cross-jurisdictional reporting ensure your program adapts swiftly to evolving compliance landscapes.

Automate the Critical, Control the Complex:

Effortless Evidence Collection
  • Automatically collect, organize, and tag evidence from key business systems such as core banking platforms, cloud environments, and human resources systems.
  • Link evidence directly to controls and regulatory requirements to eliminate last-minute audit rushes.
Framework Cross-Mapping
  • Deploy and validate controls once, then map them across multiple frameworks.
  • Reduces redundant efforts when managing regulations across different jurisdictions or subsidiaries.
Real-Time Control Testing
  • Validate control effectiveness continuously with scheduled automated tests.
  • Quickly identify and remediate compliance gaps as they emerge.

Compliance Frameworks Fintech Must Follow

Fintech organizations operate under various stringent laws and standards that govern data security, privacy, operational integrity, and financial reporting, including but not limited to:

FINRA (Financial Industry Regulatory Authority) rules for brokerage firms and exchange markets.

RBI guidelines for banking and financial institutions in India.

FFIEC standards for federal financial institution examinations.

PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) for payment security.

GLBA (Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act) for protecting consumer financial information.

ISO 27001 for information security management systems.

SOC 2 (System and Organization Controls) for data security, confidentiality, and privacy.

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) for data privacy in the EU.

TRAC Empowers Fintech Organizations To:

  • Significantly reduce the time and effort spent on audits and reporting.

  • Build stakeholder trust through robust data protection and transparent compliance processes.
